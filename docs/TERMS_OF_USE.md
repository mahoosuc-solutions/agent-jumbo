# Terms Of Use

Last updated: 2026-03-24

These terms govern use of the Agent Jumbo self-serve platform.

## Service

Agent Jumbo provides AI-assisted chat, project, workflow, queue, and operational tooling. Availability and feature scope are limited to the capabilities documented for the current release.

## Acceptable Use

You may not use Agent Jumbo to:

- break laws or regulations
- process data you are not authorized to use
- attempt unauthorized access to systems or accounts
- interfere with platform reliability or abuse shared infrastructure
- bypass trust, approval, or security controls

## Customer Configuration Responsibility

You control which providers, integrations, and credentials are configured. You are responsible for:

- the accuracy of your settings
- the data sent to model providers and integrations
- maintaining lawful rights to uploaded files and connected data
- reviewing outputs before taking material business action

## No Professional Advice

Platform outputs are software-generated assistance. They are not legal, tax, compliance, medical, or financial advice.

## Availability And Changes

Features may change between releases. Beta features are not part of the GA support commitment unless the launch inventory marks them as `ga`.

You must protect your credentials, access devices, and connected secrets. Agent Jumbo may restrict or suspend access when security or abuse concerns require it.

Paid services, if enabled, are subject to the pricing and billing flow published at the time of purchase. Third-party billing providers may impose additional terms.

The platform is provided on an as-configured basis. Customers are responsible for validating outputs, backups, deployment settings, and third-party integrations before relying on them in production.
